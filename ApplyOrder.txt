You should apply the files in this order:

namespace.yml — Creates the chat-app namespace.
mongodb-pv.yml — Creates the PersistentVolume.
mongodb-pvc.yml — Creates the PersistentVolumeClaim in the namespace.
secret.yml — Creates your secrets for JWT. (jwt to base64)
mongodb-service.yml — Exposes MongoDB for internal cluster access.
mongodb-deployment.yml — Deploys MongoDB using the PVC.
backend-deployment.yml — Deploys your backend.
backend-service.yml — Exposes your backend.
frontend-deployment.yml — Deploys your frontend.
frontend-service.yml — Exposes your frontend.

kubectl port-forward service/backend -n chat-app 5001:5001
kubectl port-forward service/frontend -n chat-app 80:80
[sudo -E kubectl port-forward service/frontend -n chat-app 80:80 (for permission)]
(use "&" at the end to run in the background in mac & linux; 
Incase of windows use differnt terminal)
